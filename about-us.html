About Us

Get to know us more, Know our history and our Team

Ukulima Agrovet Services is a leading and growing agribusiness service provider offering a wide range of services in the agricultural sector. Since 2014, we have endeavored to become centre of excellence in promoting sustainable production in the agricultural sector. We continue to invest in extensive research to ensure services offered meets the need of our target customers while delivering high quality services through use of our highly qualified and motivated staff. We are well networked both locally and globally through research institutions, industry actors, farmer and farmers organization.
